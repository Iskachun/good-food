Environmental Impact

Our choices in food production and consumption play a major role in the environmental sustainability of our planet. Learn how small changes can make a big difference in reducing our carbon footprint and conserving natural resources.



Tips for an Eco-Friendly Diet

1. Eat More Plant-Based Foods

Switching to plant-based meals can reduce your environmental impact by lowering greenhouse gas emissions, water usage, and deforestation.

2. Reduce Food Waste

Plan your meals and store food properly to minimize waste. Compost leftovers to enrich soil and avoid sending food to landfills.

3. Choose Local and Seasonal Produce

Buying locally grown and seasonal food reduces the carbon footprint caused by transportation and supports sustainable farming practices.
